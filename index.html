Enable M365 Copilot Voice Conversation:

  1. Launch Copilot App (Android, iOS).
  2. Login using your personal account (e.g., Outlook, Hotmail, Live).
  3. Tap the microphone icon located at the bottom right corner of the interface, right next to the prompt input box.
  4. Copilot may request initial configurations when used for the first time.

Cut and Paste Instructions (iOS & Android):

iOS:

  1. Select the Text:
    1. Tap and hold on the text you want to copy until you see a magnifying glass and the text is highlighted.
    2. Adjust the selection by dragging the blue dots to highlight the desired text.
  2. Copy the Text:
    1. Once the text is selected, a menu will appear.
    2. Tap on Copy.
  3. Paste the Text:
    1. Navigate to the location where you want to paste the text.
    2. Tap and hold in the text field until a menu appears.
    3. Tap on Paste to insert the text.

 

Android:

  1. Select the Text:
    1. Tap and hold on the text you want to copy. Two markers will appear, allowing you to adjust the selection. 
    2. Drag these markers to highlight the desired text.
  2. Copy the Text:
    1. Once the text is selected, a menu will appear above the selection.
    2. Tap on the Copy option.
  3. Paste the Text:
    1. Navigate to the location where you want to paste the text.
    2. Tap and hold on the text field where you want to paste the copied text.
    3. When the menu appears, tap on the Paste option.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Q:

Do I have to complete all the activities to enter the Grand Prize drawing?

A:

No! Collect a stamp for 8 of the 12 activities, show your stamp card at the Information Booth, and receive a raffle ticket for entry. Only one ticket per card and all tickets must be submitted by 2:00pm the day of the event.     

Q:

Do I need to be present to win the Grand Prize drawing. 

A:

YES! Any prizes not claimed by 5:00 PM will be forfeited.

Q:

When can I claim my prize?

A:

Redeem your winning raffle tickets at Building 8, located next to Solei & Co. between 3:00 PM and 5:00 PM on the event day, April 24, 2025.

Q:

Do I need to have Copilot installed on my phone to play?

A:

YES!  For employees and their guests to earn prizes they will need to have the Copilot app installed or can they use can use Copilot online to complete the activities. 

Q:

Can members of my family earn prizes?

A:

YES! Each member can complete their own stamp card to enter Booth drawings and/or the Grand Prize drawing. 

Q:

Am I guaranteed to win a prize from the drawing? 

A:

No!  However, there are two ways you have a chance to win; Grand Prize and Booth drawings.

Q:

Can I start with any activity/location?

A:

YES! A booklet can be picked up at any activity booth in Building 3, Rooms 1.3A-E. 

Q:

How long does the race take to complete?

A:

It’s up to you. On average it will take up to 45-60 minutes to complete 12 activities. You will be asked to complete a brief survey (https://aka.ms/RaceFeedback) when claiming your raffle ticket at the Information Booth. 

Q:

What can I win?

A:

Prizes will be announced and on display during the event. Grand Prizes are approximately $75 in value. Booth prizes are approximately $50 in value.
